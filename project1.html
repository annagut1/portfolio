Logo for a Muay Thai gym

A bold and stylish logotype created for a fictional gym White Tiger Muay Thai merges tradition with modernity. The black and white design features two symmetrical Thai tigers, forming an organic and dynamic shape. This choice symbolizes the balance and discipline of Muay Thai. The sleek typeface complements the intricate elements, resulting in a logo that encapsulates the raw power and cultural richness of the gym.
