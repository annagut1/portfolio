Packaging Design

Packaging design and logotype for an eco fair-trade craft chocolate that blends flavor and artistry. The Chili edition features a brown craft package with a white ornamental logo and a vibrant botanical illustration of chili peppers. For the Orange flavor, the same aesthetic unveils a citrusy twist with an illustration of oranges. Both designs harmonize a commitment to eco-consciousness and fair trade with the indulgence of Pure Taste's craft chocolate.
