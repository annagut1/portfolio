Harley Quinn USSR style Poster

Embracing the bold graphics of Soviet propaganda, the poster paints Harley Quinn as a symbol of Soviet superheroism. A vibrant blend of red, black, and gold, the design captures the essence of both worlds, where mischief meets the ironclad spirit of the USSR. This unique blend of pop culture and history invites viewers to contemplate the intersection of two seemingly disparate worlds. The poster stands not just as a visual spectacle but as a testament to the creative exploration that can arise when contrasting elements collide in perfect, harmonious discord.
