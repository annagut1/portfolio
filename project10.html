Childerns Book Cover

"Boba's Space Stories" is a fictive children's book that propels young readers into an interstellar adventure. Set against a dark and mystical backdrop of deep blue and blackish-green hues, the cover exudes an air of mystery and excitement. The neon yellow and purple accents add a touch of fun and playfulness to the overall atmosphere. At the heart of the cover, our lovable protagonist, a monkey astronaut, takes center stage as he's whimsically lifted by a UFO into the vastness of space. Against the backdrop of twinkling stars, this captivating scene invites children to embark on a journey filled with wonder and imagination.
